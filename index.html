Up to 8 adults can retreat to this distinctive vacation home with exquisite panoramic views of historic Old Bisbee. The spacious 2300 square foot, 4 bedroom, 2 bath home is located at the end of a winding mountain road at a mile high altitude. It includes free WiFi, mini-split heating and cooling system which allows controlled temperatures in individual rooms and an outdoor hot tub for 2 people at a time. From the private 4 car parking area, the house is down either steps or a gentle path for easier access. Dogs are welcome for a small cleaning fee and children under 6 are free.
